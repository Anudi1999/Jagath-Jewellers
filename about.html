Our Story

For over 72 years, Jagath Jewellers in Badulla has been a trusted name in fine jewellery. Our journey began with a simple promise — to offer customers genuine, high-quality gold jewellery they can trust. We proudly specialize in KT 24 and KT 22 gold jewellery, ensuring every piece reflects purity, craftsmanship, and tradition. Unlike imitation or gold-plated products, our jewellery is made from authentic gold, giving our customers lasting value and confidence in every purchase. Through generations, we have continued to serve the community with honesty, quality, and timeless designs.

Every piece at Jagath Jewellers is carefully inspected for purity and finish. We work with skilled artisans who understand both traditional Sri Lankan motifs and global trends, ensuring that your jewellery feels both familiar and unique.
